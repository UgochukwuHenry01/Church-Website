About Us

About Praise Court Assembly

Praise Court Assembly was founded by Chidebere Sunny Nwachukwu, a visionary leader dedicated to spreading faith and hope. Since its establishment, our church has been a beacon of light, guiding believers in worship, community service, and spiritual growth.

Over the years, Praise Court Assembly has grown into a thriving congregation where people experience divine transformation. Through powerful sermons, dynamic worship, and outreach programs, we continue to make an impact in lives and communities.

Our Mission

To raise a generation of spiritually empowered believers, spreading the love and truth of Christ to the world.

Our Vision

To establish a strong foundation in spiritual authority, equipping individuals with the Word and power of God to transform lives and communities.

Weekly Activities

At Praise Court Assembly, we come together every week to worship, learn, and grow in faith. Join us in our spirit-filled gatherings where lives are transformed, faith is strengthened, and the love of God is shared.

Join Us for a Life-Changing Experience

  • Sunday Worship Service: 8:00 AM - 12:00 PM - A powerful encounter with God through worship and teaching.
  • Tuesday Bible Studies: 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM - In-depth study and discussion of the Word of God.
  • Friday Deliverance Service: 6:00 PM - 9:00 PM - A night dedicated to prayer, healing, and breakthroughs.
  • Saturday Faith Clinic: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM - Strengthen your faith with biblical teachings and counseling.
