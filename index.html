About Praise Court Assembly

Praise Court Assembly was founded by Chidebere Sunny Nwachukwu, a visionary leader dedicated to spreading faith, hope, and love. Since our inception, we have served as a beacon of light—guiding believers in worship, community service, and spiritual growth.

Our church is a welcoming home for all, where lives are transformed through the power of God’s Word, heartfelt worship, and genuine fellowship. We believe in nurturing every individual’s spiritual journey and empowering them to fulfill their God-given purpose.

Over the years, Praise Court Assembly has grown into a vibrant congregation. Through inspiring sermons, dynamic worship, and impactful outreach programs, we continue to make a positive difference in the lives of our members and the wider community.

Join us as we worship, grow, and serve together—becoming a family united in faith, hope, and love.
