About Praise Court Assembly

Praise Court Assembly is a place where Jesus Christ is truly Lord. We are dedicated to spreading the gospel, uplifting lives, and creating a faith-filled community. Join us as we worship, grow in faith, and serve together.

Praise Court Assembly was founded by Chidebere Sunny Nwachukwu, a visionary leader dedicated to spreading faith and hope. Since its establishment, our church has been a beacon of light, guiding believers in worship, community service, and spiritual growth.

Over the years, Praise Court Assembly has grown into a thriving congregation where people experience divine transformation. Through powerful sermons, dynamic worship, and outreach programs, we continue to make an impact in lives and communities.
