About Praise Court Assembly

Founded by visionary leader Chidebere Sunny Nwachukwu, Praise Court Assembly is a beacon of faith, hope, and love. We guide believers through worship, community service, and spiritual growth.

Our welcoming congregation transforms lives through God's Word, heartfelt worship, and genuine fellowship. Join us in nurturing spiritual journeys and fulfilling divine purposes.
