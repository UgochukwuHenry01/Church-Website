"About Our Church"


Praise Court Assembly was founded with the mandate of establishing God's kingdom here on earth.

  • Welcome to Praise Court Assembly,
  • a community-driven church, dedicated to spreading the message of hope and
  • love. Our story began in Praise Court Assembly 12th of October 2002
  • when a group of passionate individuals
  • came together to create a place where people could connect, grow, and serve.

  • Our Mission
  • Our mission is to inspire and empower individuals
  • to live out their faith in their daily lives
  • We strive to create a welcoming environment,
  • where people can experience the love of God and
  • connect with others.
